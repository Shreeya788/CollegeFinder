HSEB Subjects

Are you looking for the subjects available after your SLC (Class : 10) ? And confused about the faculties to be chosen ?? Then, Have a look at this article.

Actually HSEB means Higher Secondary Education Board whose main office is located at Sanothimi, Bhaktapur. All the activities of +2 have been conducted by HSEB. All the colleges of Nepal are under affiliated with HSEB. After SLC, you have various options to direct and shape your career. For higher studies, you have mainly 4 faculties : Science, Management (Commerce), Education and Humanities.

  1. SCIENCE
    Subjects :
    • Compulsory English
    • Nepali (Grade- XII)
    • Computer Science
    • Mathematics
    • Chemistry (Compulsory)
    • Physics (Compulsory)
    • Biology (Zoology + Botany)

    NOTE:
    For Grade- XI Students : Computer Science and Biology are the optional subjects. So, you have to choose any of them. Mathematics is the compulsory subject.
    For Grade- XII Students ::

    You have to study Nepali in Grade- XII. You have to leave one subject : either Biology or Mathematics.

    Example : If you've taken Computer Science in Grade- XI. Then, you have to study Mathematics in Grade- XII not Biology. But, Biology students can study both Biology as well as Mathematics (additional/extra/non-credit) in Grade- XII.

    What to Choose and Career?

    As Science has a great scope in various fields, you have various options to choose depending upon your opt ional subject (Mathematics / Biology).

    If you've taken Mathematics, Engineering fields are open for you. Engineering is not limited to Civil. There are numerous Engineering fields : Civil, Electrical, Mechanical, Computer, Software Engineering etc.

    If you've taken Biology, Medical fields are welcoming you. Bio students have many choices than other students. Mainly, MBBS (Doctor), Nursing, Pharmacy, BioTechnology, Agriculture are some of them.

    You can make your choice according to your interest. Study with your own interest.

  2. MANAGEMENT(COMMERCE)
    Subjects:
    • Compulsory English
    • Nepali (Grade- XI)
    • Accountancy
    • Economics
    • Business Studies
    • Hotel Management
    • Computer Science
    • Travel and Tourism
    • Business Mathematics
    • Marketing

    NOTE:

    For Grade- XI Students : English, Nepali and Accountancy are the compulsory subjects for Grade- XI Students. Basically, Economics are also compulsory subject in many colleges. And Students are allowed to choose their optional subjects among Business Studies, Hotel Management, Computer Science etc. Few colleges offered subjects like : BOOM, Elements of Finance, Co-operative Management and others.

    For Grade- XII Students : In Grade- 12, Students have to leave Nepali Subject and instead of it, they are given a chance to choose either Business Maths or Marketing.

    Example : If one student chooses Business Studies in Grade- XI and Business Mathematics in Grade- XII, then he/she have to study English, Nepali, Account, Economics, Business Studies in Grade- XI & English, Account, Economics, Business Studies and Business Mathematics in Grade- XII respectively.

  3. Education
    Subjects:

    • English
    • Economics
    • Psychology
    • Nepali (Grade- XI)
    • Population Studies
    • Instructional Pedagogy
    • Intructional Evaluation
    • Child Development and Learning (Grade- XII)
    • Major Subject (Eng/Nep/Maths)
    • Introduction to Education

  4. Humanities
    Subjects:
    • Culture
    • English
    • Mass Communication
    • Nepali (Grade- XI)
    • Political Science
    • Sociology
    • General Law
    • Rural Development
    • Optional Subject (Eng/Nep/Maths)
